Important Formulas

Here are some important formulas you should remember:

  • Force (F) = Mass (m) × Acceleration (a)
  • Work (W) = Force (F) × Distance (d)
  • Energy (E) = Power (P) × Time (t)
  • Gravitational Force (F) = G × (m1 × m2) / r²
