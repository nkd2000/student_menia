Important Formulas

Here are some important formulas you should remember:

  • Newton's Second Law: F = ma
  • Work Done: W = Fd cos(θ)
  • Kinetic Energy: KE = 1/2 mv²
  • Potential Energy: PE = mgh

Practice Questions

Try to solve the following questions:

  1. What is the acceleration of an object if a force of 10 N is applied to a mass of 2 kg?
  2. Calculate the work done when a force of 5 N moves an object 3 meters in the direction of the force.
